What is BarCampNOLA??

BarCampNola is New Orleans’ FREE tech "unconference," organized on the fly by attendees, for attendees.

EXPLAIN MORE!!!

BarCampNola is a local incarnation of the network of user generated conferences called BarCamps. It’s an ad-hoc gathering for people to share and learn in an open environment. There are discussions, demos, and interaction from attendees, and it's free and open so each attendee can easily participate!
